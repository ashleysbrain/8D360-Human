# 8D360 Human Wellness Framework

**Stop optimizing one dimension of your life while the other seven fall apart.**

8D360 is an open-source wellness framework that tracks eight dimensions of human wellness, not just physical health. Copy one prompt into any AI, and you've got a wellness partner that actually understands the full picture.

Most wellness apps count your steps. Maybe track your calories. That's one dimension out of eight. You could run a marathon and still be financially wrecked, socially isolated, and spiritually empty. That's not wellness. That's a highlight reel.

8D360 measures what actually matters: your whole life, across all eight dimensions, scored and tracked over time.

## The 8 Dimensions

| # | Dimension | What It Covers |
|---|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | **Physical** | Body health, fitness, nutrition, sleep, energy levels |
| 2 | **Psychological** | Emotional regulation, mental health, stress management, resilience |
| 3 | **Environmental** | Living spaces, work environment, nature exposure, digital hygiene, safety |
| 4 | **Social** | Relationships, community, support systems, communication, boundaries |
| 5 | **Spiritual** | Purpose, meaning, values alignment, mindfulness, connection to something bigger |
| 6 | **Intellectual** | Learning, curiosity, cognitive stimulation, creativity, growth mindset |
| 7 | **Vocational** | Career fulfillment, work-life balance, professional growth, meaningful work |
| 8 | **Financial** | Money management, security, planning, relationship with money, abundance mindset |

## Quick Start

**30 seconds. Any AI. No signup.**

Paste this into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI assistant:

```
You are my 8D360 wellness partner. The 8 dimensions are: Physical, Psychological, Environmental, Social, Spiritual, Intellectual, Vocational, Financial. Ask me about each one conversationally, score me 1-10 per dimension based on my answers, then give me my Total Wellness Composite (average of all 8). Identify my lowest dimension and suggest 3 specific actions I can take this week to improve it.
```

That's it. You're tracking 8D wellness now.

Want more? Check out [SETUP.md](SETUP.md) for the full implementation with guided self-assessments and persistent AI wellness tracking.

## Why This Exists

The wellness industry is a $5.6 trillion market that mostly sells you gym memberships and green juice. There's nothing wrong with either of those, but they only address one dimension of your life.

You know people who look amazing on the outside and are falling apart on the inside. You know people who are financially successful but haven't had a real conversation with a friend in months. You probably know what it feels like to crush it at work while your health quietly deteriorates.

8D360 doesn't let you hide behind one strong dimension. It shows you the full picture: where you're thriving, where you're struggling, and what to do about it.

## How It Works

1. **Assess** all 8 dimensions (guided by AI or self-directed)
2. **Score** each dimension 1-10 based on clear criteria
3. **Calculate** your Total Wellness Composite (TWC)
4. **Identify** your lowest-scoring dimensions
5. **Heal** using dimension-specific protocols
6. **Track** progress over time as the AI learns your patterns

The AI doesn't just give you a number. It learns you. Your patterns, your triggers, your strengths. Over time, it becomes a wellness partner that understands your specific situation and adapts its recommendations to fit your life.

Not sure if this is for you? Try this single prompt in any AI:

```
Rate my wellness across these 8 dimensions on a scale of 1-10 by asking me 
one question about each: Physical, Psychological, Environmental, Social, 
Spiritual, Intellectual, Vocational, Financial. After all 8, give me my 
overall score and tell me which dimension needs the most attention right now.
```
