# Version History

## v1.0.0 (2026-03-23)

**Initial Release**

The 8D360 Human Wellness Framework, built for humans, by humans (with a little AI help).

### What's Included
- Complete 8-dimensional wellness framework
- 40 sub-dimensions (5 per dimension) with detailed scoring criteria
- Three-tier setup system (Instant, Full, Partner)
- AI-guided self-assessment prompt template
- Per-dimension healing protocols with actionable exercises
- Plain-language wellness guide for daily use
- Total Wellness Composite (TWC) scoring methodology

### The 8 Dimensions
1. Physical
2. Psychological
3. Environmental
4. Social
5. Spiritual
6. Intellectual
7. Vocational
8. Financial

### Design Principles
- Works with any AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, etc.)
- No app, no account, no data collection
- Designed for all humans, all neurotypes, all backgrounds
- Personalized and adaptive: the AI learns you over time
- Open source under MIT License
