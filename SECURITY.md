# Security Policy

## Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability in 8D360, please report it responsibly.

**Contact:** Ashley.Williams@divinityscience.com

**What to include:**
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if you have one)

**Response time:** We aim to acknowledge reports within 48 hours and provide a resolution timeline within 7 days.

## Data and Privacy

8D360 is a framework, not a service. It doesn't collect, store, or transmit any user data. Your wellness conversations happen between you and whatever AI assistant you choose to use.

We have no access to your scores, your assessment results, or any information you share with your AI wellness partner. Your data stays wherever you put it.

## Responsible Use

This framework is intended for personal wellness tracking and self-improvement. It is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, or clinical intervention. Please use it responsibly and seek professional help when needed.
