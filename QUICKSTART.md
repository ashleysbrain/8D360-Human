# 8D360-Human: Quick Start Guide

**Version:** 1.2.0 | **Last Updated:** March 23, 2026 | **License:** Open Source

## What is 8D360-Human?

8D360-Human is an open source wellness framework that measures your life across 8 dimensions and 40 sub-dimensions. Copy one prompt into any AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, whatever you use), and you get a guided self-assessment, personalized healing protocols, and a daily wellness plan. No app to install. No account to create.

## Who is this for?

- Anyone who wants a structured, honest look at their overall wellness
- Therapists, coaches, and counselors looking for a comprehensive assessment tool
- Researchers studying multidimensional wellness
- Developers building wellness products who need a solid framework
- People tired of one-dimensional health tracking

## Key Features

| Feature | What It Does |
|---|---|
| 8-Dimension Assessment | Scores you across Physical, Psychological, Environmental, Social, Spiritual, Intellectual, Vocational, and Financial wellness |
| 40 Sub-Dimensions | Granular breakdown within each dimension so nothing gets missed |
| AI-Guided Self-Assessment | Conversational evaluation, not a boring questionnaire |
| Healing Protocols | Targeted recommendations based on your lowest-scoring areas |
| Daily Wellness Guide | Actionable plan you can follow today, not someday |
| Works With Any AI | Not locked to one platform. Use whatever AI you already have |

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [github.com/ashleysbrain/8D360-Human](https://github.com/ashleysbrain/8D360-Human)
2. Open the main prompt file in the repo
3. Copy the entire prompt
4. Paste it into your preferred AI assistant
5. Follow the guided assessment. The AI walks you through each dimension.

That's it. You're tracking wellness across 8 dimensions in under 5 minutes.
